About Me

Hello! My name is Jennifer Shain. I am a 5th grade teacher at Stonestreet Elementary and aspire to be a school librarian. I am currently working on completing my Masters in Library Science at Eastern Kentucky University.

I enjoy working with students from diverse backgrounds and helping them find resources that drive their passion for reading. As a school librarian I will be able to provide opportunities for creativity, collaboration, and lifelong learning.

This website will provide information about my educational background,teaching experience,book recommendations, and how to contact me for tutoring services. I hope visitors find the content useful and inspiring.
Thank you for visiting my website! Feel free to reach out to me! Go to Contact Info!
